Source URL: https://example.com/ai-research-ops

Source Title: Research Operations for Small Teams

Raw Material:

Small teams often lose research value because notes remain trapped in inboxes and disconnected documents. A lightweight system should optimize for actionability first, not taxonomy. Teams benefit from short operating loops, explicit review queues, and summaries that get progressively denser over time. The key failure mode is building a perfect structure that nobody maintains.
