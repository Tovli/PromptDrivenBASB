# Executive Summary

- Small teams lose value when captured knowledge never leaves the inbox.
- Actionability beats elaborate taxonomy for long-term maintainability.
- Explicit review queues and progressive distillation reduce system drift.

# Source Material

Small teams often lose research value because notes remain trapped in inboxes and disconnected documents.

# Distillation

## Layer 2

**Small teams often lose research value because notes remain trapped in inboxes and disconnected documents.** A lightweight system should **optimize for actionability first, not taxonomy**. Teams benefit from **short operating loops, explicit review queues, and summaries that get progressively denser over time**.

## Layer 3

- ==Optimize for actionability first, not taxonomy.==
- ==Use explicit review queues and progressively denser summaries.==
