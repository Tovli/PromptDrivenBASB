# Purpose

Store the user's enduring questions so BASB can spot useful connections across notes.

# Problems

1. `How do I build self-sustaining, resilient systems that minimize fragility and reliably produce the outcomes I care about?`
2. `How can I engineer highly effective organizations by understanding how humans actually behave, decide, and respond in the real world?`
3. `What is objectively true, and how do I communicate it with absolute clarity?`
4. `How can I maximize impact relative to the time, effort, and resources I invest?`  
5. `How can I orchestrate emerging tools to amplify human capability without eroding judgment, trust, or focus?`
6. `How can I design digital systems that integrate seamlessly with physical, real-world experiences?`


# Usage Notes

- Keep the problems broad enough to span many notes.
- Prefer questions over categories.
- Update this file sparingly. It should stay stable over time.
